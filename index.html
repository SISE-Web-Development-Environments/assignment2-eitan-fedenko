About

This app was created by Eitan Fedenko as a 2nd assignment for Web Development Environments course.

I am studying at Ben-Gurion university and conveniently working as a Full-stack developer for many years.

I didn't have a lot of difficulties with the assignment because of my experience, however since I submitted it by myself, there was a difficulty with time.

I used jQuery plugin for form validation, including a custom validator for the password.

I have not used a template because I feel the point of this exercise is developing from scratch to experience the massive change in web development practices over recent years before templates and frameworks dominated.
